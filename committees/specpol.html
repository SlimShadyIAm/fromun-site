FROMUN 2016



Special Political and Decolonization Committee


SPECPOL as a matter of fact is one of the faster paced committees. With vast topic areas, agendas may range from post-war consequences to religious or cultural taboos. Logic and reasoning play their part as conclusions drawn in SPECPOL are a step ahead of theoretical possibility: it comes down to practical application and feasibility of the solutions proposed by the delegates. Which is why in this specific committee, one must not only be able to think out of the box, but also not give way under pressure, and keep all possible repercussions of one’s decision on one’s self as well as anyone and everyone who is a direct or indirect effected of what has been decided upon

Be brave, and opt for SPECPOL. You shall get plenty of opportunities to not only flaunt your debating skills, but also your ability to be diplomatic, to acknowledge, to concede, and last but not the least, to entertain. Be sure to make the debate worthwhile. Good luck delegates!
